## POST COPY:

A telecom company is losing 20% of its customers every quarter. I built a system to predict who's about to leave — and exactly when to intervene.

Here's what I found after analysing 7,043 customers:

📌 Month-to-month customers churn at 42.7% vs just 2.8% on 2-year plans
📌 The most dangerous window: customers in their first 12 months
📌 Electronic check users churn at 2.4× the rate of auto-pay customers
📌 Top 20% of high-risk customers represent $139K/month in revenue at risk

What I built:
→ Python ETL pipeline: raw CSV → cleaned data → MySQL
→ SQL cohort analysis (CTEs + window functions)
→ Logistic Regression model to score every customer's churn risk
→ Power BI executive dashboard with live risk tiers

3 recommendations delivered to "leadership":
1. Month-3 loyalty offer → projected 18% churn reduction
2. Auto-pay incentive campaign → highest ROI intervention
3. Weekly risk-score dashboard for Customer Success team
